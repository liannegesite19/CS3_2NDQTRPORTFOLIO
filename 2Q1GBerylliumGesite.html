Turon !

Chocolate Xiao Long Bao, or choco XLB, is a sweet twist on the traditional Chinese soup dumpling. Instead of savory meat filling, it has rich melted chocolate inside a soft, chewy dumpling wrapper. When steamed, the chocolate turns into a warm, gooey filling that bursts with flavor when you take a bite. It can be found in many restaurants and dessert shops, but my favorite one is from Din Tai Fung, where the texture and chocolate flavor are always perfect.

Choco XLB has been my favorite food since Grade 7 because it’s the perfect mix of comfort and surprise. What makes it so special is how it combines two very different worlds, the soft texture of dumplings and the sweetness of chocolate. It’s a fun treat to share with friends and a perfect dessert for anyone who loves trying something new. Some versions even use dark chocolate or pair it with fruit sauces to make it more exciting. Whether you eat it with chopsticks or just pick it up by hand, it’s guaranteed to make you smile!
